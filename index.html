Evolution of

Sound Players

Gramo Phone 1888 : plays records discs with grooves that are amplified by a needle.
Record Player 1925 : An instrument for reproducing sounds by means of the vibrating needle on a rotating disc.
Transistor radio 1954: a radio receiver which uses transistors to amplify the sound.
Compact Cassette 1962 : portable audio to home recording to data storage for early microcomputers.
Walkman 1979 : A small cassette player with light headphones that can be carried around to listen to music.
Discman 1983 : Its purpose was to provide quick access to electronic information on a pre-recorded disc.
MINI DISC 1992 : It is a magneto-optical disc-based audio storage and playing device offering superb playback.
IPOD 2001 : a pocket-sized portable music-playing device. It's the best-known family of MP3 players
ITouch 2007 : a portable media player, personal digital assistant, handheld gaming console, and Wi-Fi mobile device
